### Where everything went

The new assessment students page now lets you easily manage your assessment in one place.
The **Actions** menu gathers everything you do to instances in one place: Grade, Grade & close, Regrade, Change time limit, Delete, and View logs. You can choose to apply these actions to selected subsets of students.

<BlogImage
  src={actionsMenu}
  alt="The Actions menu expanded"
  caption="One menu for grading, regrading, time limits, deletes, and logs."
/>

The old "Uploads" tab is now an **Upload** menu sitting right next to Download, so importing question scores, total scores, and submissions lives next to exporting them.

<BlogImage
  src={uploadMenu}
  alt="The Upload menu expanded"
  caption="Importing scores and submissions now sits next to Download."
/>

And **View logs** drops you straight onto the assessment logs, so you can audit grading and regrading runs without digging through settings.

<BlogImage
  src={assessmentLogs}
  alt="The assessment logs page"
  caption="View logs takes you directly to the assessment activity log."
  full
/>

The "Question stats" and "Statistics" pages were merged together.

<BlogImage
  src={statistics}
  alt="The statistics page"
  caption="The merged statistics page"
  full
/>

We saved some space and closed out a couple of tabs. Best of all, there is no tab to pay: this round of screen real estate is on the house :beer:
